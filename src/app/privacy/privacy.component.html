Data privacy statement

This statement exlpains how personal data is collected and used throughout the use of our products.

Except of the data that you as a user provides, no additional personal information is gathered during the use of our products.
This data may reveal the personal financial situation of you.

Information that you provide, e.g. by creating and storing transactions or accounts, is stored locally inside the app.
After uninstalling the app, all stored information is lost. Data is always kept offline and won't be used by us outside the context of our products.
Additionally, you can delete your data at any time with the provided in-app options.

You as a user can export your data outside the app context into a separate file.
This file is not protected by us. Anyone with access to this file may also gain access to your stored app contents.
